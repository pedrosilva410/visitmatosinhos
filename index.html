Visit Matosinhos

Enjoy food and beaches in this energetic
city, part of the Porto district in Portugal

Top 3 activities to do in Matosinhos

  • Visit some of the most beautiful beaches in the Portuguese coast

    From the Matosinhos beach all the way to Angeiras there are 21 beaches to be explored

  • Go for a picnic in one of the largest parks in Portugal

    Parque da Cidade is one of the biggest parks in Portugal, from beautiful lakes and scenery to wild life there is plenty to see

  • Learn about Ocean Life at SEA LIFE Matosinhos

    With 31 Aquariums there are plenty of fishes and sea life creatures to get to know
